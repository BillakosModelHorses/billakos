This particular model horse show has an entry fee. In this page there is more information on why I chose not to make it free, some useful instructions on how you can register your horse as well as some extra helpful things you should know before entering the show.

Starting with the instructions, first you have to make a donation of $3 to my PayPal account by clicking the yellow box that directs you to the Paypal page. When donating, please add your omhps username in the comments in order for me to know who you are and add you to the entants list. Unable to do so, I won't be able to add you to the show. Afer the donation, you will have to add your username to the list on the main model horse shows page. Then I will match the username you provided me during the donation with the username on the list. Then I will add you to the show. If you don't add your username on the main model horse show page but you add it to the donation comments, don't worry, I will add it myself so I can keep track all the entries. Please see the scedule below that has all the dates and find out the period you can pay your entry fee.

There are no refunds.

The reason I am doing a paid show this time is because I want some help in order to maintain and improve this blog/website. My goal is not to pocket the money, but to use it to enhance this space for everyone. Your contributions will go directly toward covering the necessary fees and expenses I have to pay to keep the site running. Any extra money will be donated to a wonderful local charity, which provides care and support for equine animals in need: www.espihelphorses.org.

Because this is a premium show, I will also send the winners virtual rosettes/cards as a bonus for their models achievments! The winners can print them and keep them in their placing files. Who gets virtual rossetes/cards? For Classes - the top 6. For Sections - the top 3 (Section champion, Section reserve champion, Section reserve to reserve champion). For divisions - the top 3 (Division champion, Division reserve champion, Division reserve to reserve champion). The top 2 in each class will qualify.
