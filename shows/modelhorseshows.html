Welcome to the BMH model horse shows!

Welcome to my online model horse show series! I’ve decided to start hosting my own shows using the omhps.com platform. Most of these shows will be free, and in the future, I may host a benefit show to support a local charity here in Greece. Please note that I am not experienced in hosting shows, but I believe it’s always good to try something new! This will be an ongoing show series, and I may even host finals for the champions of previous shows. You can find all the concluded shows at the bottom of this page. Click any image to view its results, which will take you to the OMHPS show page. Have fun!

You can also ckeck the rest of my website. There is a blog, an open chat and even a Breyer related mini game.
