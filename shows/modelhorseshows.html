Welcome to the BMH model horse shows!

Welcome to my online model horse show series! I’ve decided to start hosting my own shows using the omhps.com platform. There will be both free and paid shows to support a local charity here in Greece. Please note that I am not the most experienced in hosting shows (I have hosted a couple), but I believe it’s always good to try and learn something new! This will be an ongoing show series, and I may even host finals for the champions of previous shows. You can find all the concluded shows at the bottom of this page. Click any image to view its results, which will take you to the OMHPS show page. Have fun!

You can also ckeck the rest of my website. There is a blog, an open chat and even a Breyer related mini game.
